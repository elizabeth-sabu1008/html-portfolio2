Best Movies According To Elizabeth

My Top Three Movies Of All Time!

Padakallam

A wonderful sci-fic movie that revolves around college students and their professors.

Premalu

A movie depicting life of people who live in hyderabad and also a love story between sachin and reenu.

Banglore days

A story revolving around cousins who have their best dream life in banglore and also going through their personal problems in their relationship.
